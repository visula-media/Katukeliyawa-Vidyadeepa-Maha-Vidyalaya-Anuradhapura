About Us

Nurturing Young Minds

A Tradition of Excellence & Discipline

Katukeliyawa Vidyadeepa Maha Vidyalaya is a respected government school located in Anuradhapura, Sri Lanka, providing quality education from Grade 1 to Grade 11. Our school is dedicated to nurturing young minds in a safe, disciplined, and supportive learning environment.

We focus on building a strong academic foundation while also promoting moral values, leadership, and personal growth. With the guidance of committed and experienced teachers, our students are encouraged to think critically, act responsibly, and strive for excellence in all aspects of life.

At Katukeliyawa Vidyadeepa Maha Vidyalaya, we believe education is not only about knowledge, but also about shaping character and preparing students to become confident and responsible citizens of the future.

Education

Academic Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed for outstanding achievement and continuous personal development.

Primary Education

Grades 1-5 focus on building a strong foundation in literacy, numeracy, and establishing core moral values in a nurturing atmosphere.

Junior Secondary

Grades 6-9 broaden students' perspectives with critical subjects, fostering analytical thinking and independent learning skills.

Ordinary Level (O/L)

Grades 10-11 offer rigorous preparation for national examinations with expert guidance across science, arts, and commerce streams.

Beyond Academics

Extracurricular Activities

We believe in holistic development. Beyond the classroom, we foster physical health, creativity, and leadership.

Sports & Athletics

Promoting physical well-being, teamwork, and leadership through cricket, volleyball, netball, and track & field athletics.

Clubs & Societies

Developing specific interests, public speaking, and organizational skills via debating, science, and environmental clubs.

Aesthetic Studies

Fostering creativity and emotional intelligence through traditional music, dancing, and art, honoring Sri Lankan traditions.
